Two Hats Prisoner Guessing Game

Prisoner One

Click the prisoners to change hat color



Backstory:

You are a warden working at a high security prison when two felons comes into the prison. You hate rule breakers so you are about to send them to the electric chair when you remember your mothers words, "try giving others a second chance".

You decide to follow your mom's message but with a twist, they have to play a game. You will place either a red or blue hat on their heads, and they must not look at what color they are. The two prisoners will have one guess each and combined must get exactly one of the two guesses right to live. They can not communicate, only guess based on the other prisoner's hat.
